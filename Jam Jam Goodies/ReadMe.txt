Contents

- WAV and OGG versions of the original song "Nu Horizons" by "The Darkhorse Collective."

- PDF of the lyrics written by Tyler Klivickis.

- PDF and GP files for the guitar and bass tablature/sheet music written by Aaron Baer, Tyler Klivickis and Ari Shellist.

- MIDI data for guitar and bass.

- Seperate WAV tracks for guitars, bass, drum mics, percussion, vocals, and synths all same length of the whole song.

You are free to use any of the contents in this folder as you see fit for the development of your games. Any DAW such as Audacity, Pro Tools, Logic and etc. can edit the seperate music tracks and MIDI provided.
